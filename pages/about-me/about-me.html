Growing Up

I spent my childhood in Kentucky. Being a creative kid, I had to get out of Kentucky. Kansas City Art Insitute was where I landed for my freshman year of college--but during my time there, my older brother was diagnosed with terminal cancer. I returned home, and my brother's life was cut short. After my brother's passing, my studies continued at The School of The Art Insitute of Chicago, where I obtained a Bachelor's of Fine Arts in Studio Art.

Being Creative

Creativity comes natural to humanity. A few things fascinated me as a young boy, the color green and drawing were among them. To my parent's dismay, I also enjoyed taking things apart and seeing how they worked--only later in life did I learn how to reassemble things.

Brief Professional Bio

Since graduating from the School of The Art Insitute of Chicago in 2017, I have lived in Los Angeles, California. I have worked on 100 different film/tv/commercial productions. I've filled various roles, from set-building and truck driving to principal talent and director.
